Until I Can Call Her "Love"...Again

A book of regrets, love, and suffering

Prologue – The Fall

I thought desire was harmless fire, a moment that would pass. But fire burns, and now I hold the ashes of your trust in trembling hands.

The Wounds

There is a scratch on my skin, a mark left in anger — but it pales beside the wound I carved in your heart. My body will heal. But you? I pray I have time to help stitch the soul I tore open.

A Fragile Night

"Can we pretend it didn’t happen, just for tonight?" you whispered. We reached for each other, two broken hearts clutching at what was once whole. But even in your arms, I felt the ghost of my mistake watching us.

Written with regret, patience, and hope.
