A Prayer in the Shadows

A daily meditation of repentance, hope, and devotion

I kneel before the altar,
though night still clings to my bones.
I lift my hands, shaking,
and whisper the words I cannot say aloud.

"Come near to God and he will come near to you." - James 4:8

Lord, forgive the man I was,
who strayed into temptation,
who broke the trust of the one You gave me,
who let desire blind his eyes
and pride harden his heart.

"If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness." - 1 John 1:9

I pray for mercy,
though I feel none for myself.
I pray for strength,
though my knees are weak from shame.
I pray for her
the one I have wounded,
whose love was a mirror
I shattered with careless hands.

"Be kind and compassionate to one another, forgiving each other, just as in Christ God forgave you." - Ephesians 4:32

Every day I rise,
I bow, I fall, I pray again.
Even if the earth crumbles beneath me,
even if my body dies before my soul
has learned humility,
I will call on You.

"The Lord is near to the brokenhearted and saves the crushed in spirit." - Psalm 34:18

Let my tears water the soil of repentance,
let my sorrow shape the clay of my heart,
let my longing for forgiveness
become a ladder to Your grace.

"Create in me a clean heart, O God, and renew a right spirit within me." - Psalm 51:10

Guide my words, Lord,
that they may be soft enough to heal,
my silence, that it may give her peace,
my patience, that it may prove my love.

"A gentle answer turns away wrath, but a harsh word stirs up anger." - Proverbs 15:1

Even in suffering,
I will not despair.
Even in solitude,
I will not stop seeking You.
For You are the light that pierces the shadows,
the river that cleanses the stain of sin,
the voice that whispers,
“Rise again, my child.”

"Cast all your anxiety on him because he cares for you." - 1 Peter 5:7

I pray for her trust to return,
for her heart to find comfort,
for my own spirit to learn humility,
and for both of us to walk in Your ways.

"Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding." - Proverbs 3:5

Even if the night stretches endless,
even if my own hands must carry the weight of my mistakes,
I will pray —
until the heavens hear,
until my soul is quiet,
until I can stand once more
and call her love,
with a heart that is unbroken and pure.

"I can do all things through him who strengthens me." - Philippians 4:13

Written with devotion, repentance, and hope.
